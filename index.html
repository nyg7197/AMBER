AMBER

Nicolas Yan Garcia

amber is a web based iteration

AMBER is a multi-media installation that poses the question: “In the digital era that gets increasingly easier to create constructs of the self, do we have agency in how we are preserved and remembered?” Through the manipulation of archival images and videos, AMBER explores the suffocating permanence of being endlessly documented in the digital age.
